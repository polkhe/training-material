>    > ### {% icon tip %} Tip: Rename an history
>    >
>    > 1. Click on **Unnamed history** (or the name of the history) (**Click to rename history**) on the top of your history
>    > 2. Write the new name
>    > 3. Type on <kbd>Enter</kbd>
>    {: .tip}
